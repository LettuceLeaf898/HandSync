About Hand-Sync

We created Hand-Sync to help people improve both their rhythm skills and their hand-eye coordination skills. By formatting it as a game, we hope to make building and fine-tuning base motor skills a little more fun for everyone!

We challenged ourselves to learn something new while working on this project, and aimed to make a program that was both fun and accessible through the use of cybernetics and computer vision. All you need to play Hand-Sync is a computer with a camera!
